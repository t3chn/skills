# Google Drive Integration

URL: https://www.greptile.com/docs/google-drive-integration

Connect Google Drive to give Greptile access to your documentation, PRDs, and specs during code reviews.

## What You Get

* **PRDs and specifications** stored in Google Docs
* **Architecture diagrams** and technical documentation
* **Meeting notes** and decision records
* **Shared spreadsheets** with requirements or configurations

## Setup

Go to Integrations

Click **Integrations** in the left sidebar of your Greptile dashboard.

Connect Google Drive

Click **Connect** on the Google Drive card.

Authorize Access

1. Choose a Google account to connect.

2. Click **Continue** to proceed.

3. Select the checkbox to allow Greptile access to your Drive files.

Done

Google Drive appears as a connected data source in your dashboard.
